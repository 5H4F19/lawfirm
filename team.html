Team

Our team consists of lawyers who are each regarded as leaders amongst their peers and who will provide best-in class service on competitive terms. We are the only firm that boasts multiple ranked lawyers in Legal 500, IFLR 1000, and Chambers Legal.

Our firm includes a team of veteran Saudi Arabian and internationally qualified lawyers who assist on a wide range of practice areas, which includes Corporate & Regulatory (covering private and public mergers and acquisitions, joint ventures and complex corporate restructurings), Dispute Resolution (both arbitration and litigation, and investigations), Finance & Projects (including project finance and Islamic finance transactions), Equity and Debt Capital Markets (including IPOs and sukuks), and Compliance and Corporate Governance (covering competition and anti-trust, data privacy, cybersecurity, and board responsibilities). Our team is able to structure, draft, negotiate and execute transactions and has the track record to do so. The combination of our international qualifications and local legal knowledge provides us with an edge over any international/local combination of firms and allows us to be the only team that would be needed on most transactions. The vast majority of our lawyers are professionally fluent in Arabic and English, which allows us to work on a wider scope of transactions involving due diligence of Arabic and English documents, drafting Arabic and English legislation for government clients, bespoke agreements, prospectuses and disclosures for the public securities markets.
